• start: start collecting the current position of your device. The collected data is stored locally. The current position is stored only if the distance to the previous position is > 10m. A sample is taken every 5 seconds.
  • stop: stop collecting data. Paint the trip onto a google map
  • save: you can save your trip to my server anonymously. Beware that other people can load and see it
  • load all trips: show a list of saved trips
  • places: show a list of places in your vicinity
